Who I Am

Engineer. Founder. Visionary.

I am the founder of 7X Syria Ltd. — a Syrian technology company dedicated to building secure, open-source communication tools. My work focuses on end-to-end encrypted messaging, Android development, and Matrix protocol technologies.

With expertise in Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Rust, and distributed systems, I lead the development of Alttyar, a privacy-first Matrix messaging platform for Android.
